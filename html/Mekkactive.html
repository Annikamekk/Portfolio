June 2023

Project info

An acquaintance of mine just became self-employed and asked if I could make a logo to brighten up his name. Of course I did that with love! At first I came up with things that I liked, but he didn't think that was something for his company, namely kitesurfing and roofing, and gave reasons for what he thought could be done.

This was also my first assignment outside of my studies and I immediately enjoyed doing something different. I didn't put much time into it, especially with sketches, I do want to put more time into that in the future.

Client: Self employed person.

Challenge: Make a logo that has to do something with kitesurfing and roofing.

Tools: Illustrator.
